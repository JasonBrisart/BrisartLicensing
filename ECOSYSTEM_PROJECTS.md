# Brisart Ecosystem Projects

This file lists Brisart ecosystem projects and their general purpose.

---

## Current Projects

### BrisartDevTools

Developer tools for local-first project workflows, automation, documentation, release preparation, and development support.

---

### BrisartResearchArchive

Research archive software for structured framework work, research organization, archival workflows, and experimental evaluation.

---

### BrisartPreservationTools

Preservation-focused tools for archive verification, file integrity, long-term storage workflows, and local preservation systems.

---

### BrisartIdentityTools

Identity-focused tools for local identity records, offline authentication experiments, physical token verification, and auditable credential workflows.

---

### BrisartLicensing

The central licensing, policy, subscription, donation, and ecosystem participation repository for Brisart software.

---

## Future Projects

Future Brisart projects may be added as the ecosystem grows.

The goal of the ecosystem is to support local-first, auditable, maintainable, and long-term research environments.
