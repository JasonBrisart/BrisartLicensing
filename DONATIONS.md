# Donations

Donations help support continued development of the Brisart software ecosystem.

The Brisart ecosystem includes software projects focused on local-first, auditable, maintainable, and long-term research environments.

---

## Purpose

Donations may help support:

- Continued software development
- New ecosystem tools
- Research and experimentation
- Long-term maintenance
- Documentation improvements
- Archival tooling
- Preservation tooling
- Identity tooling
- Development workflow tooling

---

## What Donations Support

Donations support the ongoing work of building and maintaining Brisart ecosystem software.

This may include projects such as:

- BrisartDevTools
- BrisartResearchArchive
- BrisartPreservationTools
- BrisartIdentityTools

and future Brisart projects.

---

## Donations Are Not Subscriptions

A donation is not the same as a subscription.

Donations do not provide:

- Technical support
- Help desk access
- Consulting services
- Managed services
- Guaranteed feature development
- Guaranteed implementation requests
- Guaranteed timelines

Donations are voluntary support for continued ecosystem development.

---

## Why Donate

Brisart software is built to support local-first, auditable, and long-term maintainable research environments.

Donations help make continued development possible.

If the Brisart ecosystem is useful, interesting, or aligned with your own requirements, donations are one way to support future work.
